Solar Energy System

Overview Benefits Planning Installation Maintenance

Overview

Solar energy takes the light (also known as an electromagnetic radiation) emitted by the sun and turns it into energy that can be used in homes. Solar energy can decrease the cost of electricity and is efficient on both small and large scales. It can even be used during the nighttime if storage is installed with it.

Benefits

  1. Decrease in electricity bill: Solar energy can save homeowners a considerable amount of money in electricity bills.
  2. Increased home value: Homes with solar panels are worth $20 more for each dollar invested in solar panels. Homeowners can increase their home's selling price by 4%.
  3. Helping the environment: Solar energy minimizes the use of fossil fuels for electricity in homes, which helps the environment.
  4. Low-maintenance expenses: Solar panels require very little maintenance except when damaged by natural causes. Otherwise, they can work for many years without anything besides the occasional cleaning.
  5. Long-term savings: Homeowners can break on the investment of solar panels in 6-10 years, which is before the panels need to be replaced or need extreme repairs.

Planning

First you should determine Your Home's Energy Efficiency and apply any measures to decrease energy use before choosing solar. Next you should assess your solar potential. More information on how to do so is provided on this website. Then you should estimate your home's solar electricity needs with a professional.

Installation

Once you have determined that a microhydropwer system works for you, you can start the installation process. It would be best to work with a licensed installer to set up the system properly. Micro hydro power systems cost between $1,500 and $4,000 per kilowatt installed capacity, although the cost depends also on your site. Although this might be expensive, it will be worth it in the long run. Plus, there may be financial incentives for using a renewable energy system in your area, which can decrease the cost.

Maintenance

Solar panels require minimal maintenance but to maximize their efficiency you should check once in a while for buildup, and cleaning them 2 or more times each year.
